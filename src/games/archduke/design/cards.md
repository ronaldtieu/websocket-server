# Archduke Cards

## Card Types:

### Number Cards
- point values ranging from -3 to 13
- can be matched by value (same number)

### Action Cards (Face Cards)
when discarded or matched, grant special abilities:

#### PEEK
- look at any one card on the table
- can be yours or another player's card

#### GIVE
- give a penalty card from the draw pile to any other player
- receiving player adds it to their set without looking
- can cause players to have more than 4 cards

#### SWAP
- swap positions of any two cards on the table (one-for-one)
- steal another player's card in exchange for your own
- or swap one card each from two opposing players
- no player (including the swapper) may look at their new card

### Special Cards

#### ARCHDUKE
- only card worth negative points (-3)
- most valuable card in the game

#### ECLIPSE
- some are worth 0 points
- others are worth 13 points
- same symbol indicates they can be matched with each other
- do not have an associated action

## Matching Rules

- number cards match by value (same number)
- face cards match by symbol
- matching is the only way to get rid of cards
- when a card is matched, it creates an empty space in the player's set
